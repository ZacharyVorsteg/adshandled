FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

What's included in the $299/month?
Everything. Facebook ads, Instagram ads, Google Search ads, ad creative and copywriting, campaign setup, weekly optimization, A/B testing, monthly reports, and a dedicated account manager. The only thing not included is the ad spend itself — that goes directly to Google/Meta and you control the budget.
How much should I spend on ads?
We recommend starting with $500-1,000/month in ad spend for most small businesses. This gives us enough budget to test, optimize, and deliver meaningful results. You can increase your budget as you see returns. The ad spend goes directly to Google/Meta — we never mark it up.
Is there a contract?
No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. We keep clients because we deliver results, not because of fine print. If you're not happy, you can cancel with zero hassle.
How fast will I see results?
Your ads go live within 48 hours of signing up. Most clients start seeing leads within the first week. It typically takes 2-4 weeks to fully optimize campaigns for peak performance as we gather data on what works best for your specific market.
What industries do you work with?
We work with local service businesses of all kinds — plumbers, HVAC, roofers, electricians, dentists, restaurants, auto shops, landscapers, cleaning services, and more. If your customers search for your services online, we can help.
How is this so affordable?
We use AI-powered tools to handle the repetitive parts of ad management — audience research, bid optimization, performance monitoring — which lets us deliver premium results without the premium price tag. You get the same quality as a $2,000/month agency for a fraction of the cost.
Do I need to provide anything?
Just access to your Facebook Business page and/or Google Ads account (we'll help you set these up if you don't have them). We handle everything else — creative, copy, targeting, optimization.
What if I've tried ads before and they didn't work?
That's actually most of our clients. DIY ads and cheap automation tools waste money because they lack proper targeting, creative, and optimization. We build campaigns from scratch with proven strategies for your specific industry. If it doesn't work, you cancel — remember, no contracts.
