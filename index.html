Why This Market, Right Now

Post-pandemic logistics reshuffling has created sustained demand for West Palm Beach industrial. Proximity to Port of Miami, Fort Lauderdale Airport, and the I-95 corridor creates structural supply constraints. Tenant credit quality is improving. Institutional capital is repricing older assets — creating an opportunity window for experienced sponsors.

  • Florida tax policy favorable to commercial depreciation
  • Amazon/e-commerce logistics hub expansion ongoing
  • Institutional cap rate compression = investor entry point
  • 3.2% population growth driving commercial demand
